About Me

I am a Stony Brook University alumnus with a BS in Human Evolutionary Biology and a minor in Geospatial Science. I am passionate about cartographic design, geospatial information systems (GIS), computer-based biostatitsics, coding, and conservation biology. My passions derive from watching nature documentaries since childhood, such as Steve Irwin's iconic The Crocodile Hunter. I grew up along the beaches of Southeastern North Carolina where I would spend my time surfing, learning, and adventuring. I subsequently moved to New York to pursue my degree and to further explore our beautiful world.
